# Creamy Tuscan Chicken

Restaurant-quality Italian chicken that's easier than you think!

## Ingredients

- 1 large chicken breast (6-8 oz)
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes
- 1 cup fresh spinach
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
- Olive oil
- Italian seasoning
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Season and sear chicken breast until golden (6-7 min per side)
2. Remove chicken and set aside
3. Saut√© garlic and sun-dried tomatoes
4. Add cream, parmesan, and Italian seasoning
5. Add spinach and wilt
6. Return chicken to pan and simmer in sauce
7. Serve with pasta or vegetables

## Tips

- Pound chicken to even thickness for consistent cooking
- Use the flavorful oil from sun-dried tomatoes jar
- Don't let cream boil - keep it at a gentle simmer
