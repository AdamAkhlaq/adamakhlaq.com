# Crispy Breakfast Tacos

Perfect way to start your morning with a satisfying crunch!

## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 2 small flour tortillas
- 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 2 tbsp butter
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh cilantro
- Salsa (optional)

## Instructions

1. Whisk eggs with salt and pepper
2. Scramble eggs in butter until fluffy
3. Warm tortillas and fill with eggs and cheese
4. Fold and crisp in pan until golden
5. Top with cilantro and salsa

## Tips

- Use real butter for best flavor
- Don't overcook the eggs - keep them slightly soft
- Press down gently while crisping for even browning
