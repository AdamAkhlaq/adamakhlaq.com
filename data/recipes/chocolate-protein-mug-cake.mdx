# Chocolate Protein Mug Cake

Your fastest path to chocolate satisfaction - with 20g of protein!

## Ingredients

- 1 scoop chocolate protein powder (30g)
- 2 tbsp flour (or oat flour)
- 1/4 tsp baking powder
- 3 tbsp milk (any kind)
- 1 egg white
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
- Optional: sugar-free chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Mix all dry ingredients in a microwave-safe mug
2. Add milk, egg white, and vanilla
3. Stir until smooth
4. Microwave for 90 seconds to 2 minutes
5. Let cool for 1 minute before eating

## Tips

- Don't overmix - some lumps are okay
- Watch closely - microwaves vary in power
- Top with Greek yogurt for extra protein
- Add a pinch of instant coffee for mocha flavor
