# Thai Peanut Noodles

A quick, flavorful meal that comes together in under 25 minutes!

## Ingredients

- 4 oz rice noodles
- 2 tbsp peanut butter
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tsp sriracha
- 1/2 cup julienned carrots
- 1/2 cup bell pepper strips
- 2 green onions, sliced
- Crushed peanuts for garnish
- Fresh cilantro

## Instructions

1. Cook rice noodles according to package directions
2. Mix peanut butter, soy sauce, lime juice, and sriracha with pasta water
3. Stir-fry vegetables quickly (2-3 minutes)
4. Toss noodles with sauce and vegetables
5. Top with peanuts, cilantro, and green onions

## Tips

- Use some hot pasta water to thin the peanut sauce
- Don't overcook the vegetables - they should be crisp-tender
- Add more sriracha if you like it spicy!
